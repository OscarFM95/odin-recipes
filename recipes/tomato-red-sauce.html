Tomato red sauce

Ingredients

  • 13 Green tomatoes
  • 2 tree chili
  • 4 guajillo chili
  • 1 black chili
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup of water

Instructions

  1. Cook the green tomatoes in a pan
  2. Cook the chili in a pan
  3. Inside a blender we put the cooked chili, the water and salt
  4. Blend the ingredients
  5. We add the cooked tomatoes to the blender
  6. We blend again
  7. The sauce is ready. Enjoy!
